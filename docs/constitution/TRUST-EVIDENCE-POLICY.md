# Trust Evidence Policy

**Constitution Version:** v1.0

## Activation rule

No trust signal may become `Active` without a recorded evidence evaluation satisfying its trust type's sources, weights, integrity rules, recency rules, minimum evidence, and calculation basis. Failed integrity is fail-closed and cannot be offset by evidence weight.

## Evaluation and traceability

An evaluation records the trust ID, subject, context, linked standing, linked claim, each evidence source, normalized weight, integrity result, observed and expiry times, calculation version, evaluator, resulting confidence, and decision references. Conflicting evidence must be preserved and resolved explicitly. A calculation is deterministic for the same governed inputs and policy version; confidence may not be inferred from class, ownership, or standing alone.
