## Valid delegation statuses

A delegation record may carry one of the following statuses: Pending, Active, Expired, Revoked, Retired.

## Delegation doctrine

Delegation transfers voting expression only. Delegation does not transfer authority ownership. A delegated vote is constitutionally attributed to the delegating actor and the delegate equally. Delegation chains are not permitted; a delegate may not re-delegate.
