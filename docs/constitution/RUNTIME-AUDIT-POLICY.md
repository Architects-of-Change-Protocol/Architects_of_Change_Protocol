# Runtime Audit Policy

**Constitution Version:** v1.0

## Constitutional Rule

Every runtime action must be auditable.

## Audit domains

**Execution Audit** — Verification that executions were authorized, scoped, and completed within constitutional bounds.

**Evidence Audit** — Verification that required evidence was generated, is complete, and is integrity-preserved.

**Integrity Audit** — Verification of authenticity, completeness, traceability, immutability, and verifiability across all runtime records.

**Consumption Audit** — Verification that capability consumption was recorded accurately and settled under the governing economic policy.

**Compliance Audit** — Verification that all execution obligations, evidence obligations, settlement obligations, and policy constraints were satisfied.
