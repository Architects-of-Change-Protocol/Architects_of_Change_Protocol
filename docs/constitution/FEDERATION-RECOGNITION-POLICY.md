# Federation Recognition Policy

**Constitution Version:** v17.0

## Purpose

Recognition determines whether a sovereign constitutional system is acknowledged as a valid participant in a federation relationship. Recognition is constitutionally distinct from trust, delegation, and authority transfer.

## Valid recognition states

A recognition record may occupy one of the following states: Unknown, Observed, Recognized, Restricted, Suspended, Revoked, Retired.
