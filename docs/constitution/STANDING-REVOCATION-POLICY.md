# Standing Revocation Policy

**Constitution Version:** v8.0

## Valid causes

- `Fraud`
- `Identity Failure`
- `Eligibility Failure`
- `Evidence Failure`
- `Constitutional Override`
- `Voluntary Withdrawal`

Revocation permanently removes the constitutional right to participate while preserving history. It requires a revocable standing authority, valid cause, traceable evidence, responsible revoking authority, ratified Type B or Type C amendment, effective date, and `Revoked` status. Revocation may transition only to retirement and may not be undone by revalidation.

No standing revocations are recorded at v5.0.

## Authority chain

The revoking authority must trace to the standing owner or a constitutionally superior authority. Voluntary withdrawal requires authenticated participant evidence but does not permit deletion of the standing record.
