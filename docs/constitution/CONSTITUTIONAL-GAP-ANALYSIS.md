# Constitutional Gap Analysis

## Preamble

This document records all identified constitutional gaps, their severity, remediation status, and closure evidence. A gap is a missing, incomplete, or inconsistent constitutional artifact, authority, scanner, test, lifecycle record, or policy. The Constitutional Gap Analysis must show no open Critical or High gaps before Level 3 certification may be claimed.
