# Standing Representation Policy

**Constitution Version:** v10.0

## Constitutional rule

Representation answers who may act on behalf of a participant. Representation never transfers ownership or authority, never enlarges the represented participant's standing, always expires, is scope-bound, and is auditable. The representative must possess active standing appropriate to the represented act.
