## Valid motion statuses

A motion record may carry one of the following statuses: Draft, Prepared, Open, Closed, Resolved, Retired.

## Motion fields

Every motion must contain: Motion ID, Governance Reference, Voting Authority, Eligibility Policy, Weight Policy, Delegation Allowed, Open Date, Close Date, Outcome Rule, Status.
