# Claim Authorities

**Constitution Version:** v7.0

## Constitutional rule

Only claim types in this catalog may be submitted to governed decisions. Claim IDs are permanent and unique. Retirement preserves the record and never reassigns its identity.

## Catalog integrity

Owners must be constitutional authorities. Evidence policies must exist and cover exactly one claim type. Creation, semantic expansion, retirement, or ownership transfer requires a ratified Type B or Type C amendment.
