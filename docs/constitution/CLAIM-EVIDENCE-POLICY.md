# Claim Evidence Policy

**Constitution Version:** v8.0

## Acceptance rule

No claim may enter `Accepted` until every required-evidence and minimum-evidence condition for its claim type is satisfied from allowed sources. Evidence must remain integrity-protected and traceable for the full audit life of the claim.

## Evidence quality

Evidence quality is evaluated for relevance, provenance, integrity, recency, independence, and completeness. A higher quantity of lower-quality records does not automatically satisfy or supersede a minimum. Evidence failures trigger `CLM-V-003` or `CLM-V-008`; missing provenance triggers `CLM-V-009`.
