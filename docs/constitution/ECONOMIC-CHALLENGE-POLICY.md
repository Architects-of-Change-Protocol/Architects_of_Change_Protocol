# Economic Challenge Policy

**Constitution Version:** v16.0

## Challenge grounds

An economics challenge may be initiated on one or more of the following grounds:

- **Invalid Right** — The claimed economic right is not cataloged or has been revoked.
- **Invalid Obligation** — The asserted economic obligation is not cataloged or has been revoked.
- **Invalid Asset** — The referenced asset class or asset record is not cataloged or has been revoked.
- **Invalid Consumption** — The consumption record does not satisfy the governing consumption policy.
- **Invalid Settlement** — The settlement record does not satisfy the governing settlement policy.
- **Invalid Treasury Action** — The treasury action does not satisfy the governing treasury policy.
- **Invalid Valuation** — The valuation record does not satisfy the governing valuation model.
- **Economic Fraud** — The economics act was produced through fraudulent records or false evidence.
- **Evidence Failure** — Required evidence is absent, tampered, or insufficient.
