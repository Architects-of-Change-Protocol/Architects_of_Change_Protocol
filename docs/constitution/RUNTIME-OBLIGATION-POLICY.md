# Runtime Obligation Policy

**Constitution Version:** v17.0

## Obligation dimensions

**Execution Obligations** — Every authorized execution must be carried out within the authorized scope, window, and capability limits.

**Evidence Obligations** — Every execution must generate the required evidence types before status may advance to `Completed`.

**Settlement Obligations** — Every execution that consumes economic resources must produce a settlement record under the governing economic settlement policy.

**Audit Obligations** — Every execution must be auditable by any authorized audit authority.

**Compliance Obligations** — Every execution must satisfy all applicable policy constraints.
