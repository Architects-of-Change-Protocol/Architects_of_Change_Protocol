# Economic Revocation Policy

**Constitution Version:** v16.0

## Revocation causes

An economics authority or economics act may be revoked on one or more of the following causes:

- **Fraud** — The economics act was produced through fraudulent records or false evidence.
- **Invalid Right** — The claimed economic right is not cataloged or has been revoked.
- **Invalid Obligation** — The asserted economic obligation is not cataloged or has been revoked.
- **Invalid Settlement** — The settlement record does not satisfy the governing settlement policy.
- **Treasury Violation** — The treasury action violates the governing treasury policy.
- **Constitutional Override** — A constitutional amendment revokes the economics authority.
- **Governance Decision** — A legitimate governance decision revokes the economics authority.
