# Federation Violation Catalog

**Constitution Version:** v16.0

## Violation catalog

| Violation ID | Violation Name | Severity | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| FED-V-001 | Unauthorized Federation | Critical | A federation relationship was created without a valid cataloged federation authority |
| FED-V-002 | Invalid Recognition | Critical | A federation recognition record is invalid, fraudulent, or lacks required evidence |
| FED-V-003 | Invalid Trust | High | A trust level assignment violates the governing trust policy |
| FED-V-004 | Invalid Delegation | High | A delegation record violates the governing delegation policy |
| FED-V-005 | Delegation Beyond Scope | High | A delegation grants authority beyond the permitted scope of the delegation policy |
| FED-V-006 | Sovereignty Transfer | Critical | A federation act purports to transfer sovereignty from one constitutional system to another |
| FED-V-007 | Invalid Governance Federation | High | A federated governance act violates the governance policy or overrides local sovereignty |
| FED-V-008 | Invalid Lifecycle Transition | High | A federation lifecycle transitioned to a state not permitted from the current state |
| FED-V-009 | Invalid Revocation | High | A federation was revoked without valid cause, evidence, or decision reference |
| FED-V-010 | Invalid Challenge | High | A challenge was raised on grounds not permitted by the challenge policy |
| FED-V-011 | Federation Integrity Failure | Critical | A federation record cannot be verified as unaltered or traceable |
| FED-V-012 | Federation Traceability Failure | High | A federation act lacks a complete traceable record to its authority, recognition, and trust |
| FED-V-013 | Federation Escalation | High | A federation act was used to claim authority beyond the scope of the federation authority |
| FED-V-014 | Sovereignty Boundary Failure | Critical | A federation act violated the sovereignty boundary of a participating constitutional system |
