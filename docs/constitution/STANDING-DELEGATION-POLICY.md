# Standing Delegation Policy

**Constitution Version:** v16.0

## Constitutional rule

Delegation permits a qualified participant to exercise only the delegable participation scope recorded for a standing authority. It never creates ownership, expands authority, bypasses eligibility, or survives suspension, revocation, retirement, or expiry. Constitutional standing and runtime claimant, verifier, witness, trust, and consent standing are non-delegable.
