# Claim Withdrawal Policy

**Constitution Version:** v10.0

## Constitutional rule

Withdrawal is permitted only for a withdrawable accepted claim and must be authorized by the `Claim Owner`, an `Authorized Representative`, or a `Constitutional Override`. Representation must be traceable to active standing and its authorization evidence.

Withdrawal changes lifecycle state; it does not delete or rewrite the claim, evidence, disputes, decisions, or history. Claims remain auditable forever and transition from `Withdrawn` only to `Retired`.
