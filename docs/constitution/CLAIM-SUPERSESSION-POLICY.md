# Claim Supersession Policy

**Constitution Version:** v1.0

## Constitutional rule

A supersedable accepted or disputed claim may be replaced only when the old and new claims address the `Same Subject` and at least one authority basis applies: `Higher Evidence Quality`, `More Recent Evidence`, `Constitutional Override`, or `Decision Authority`.

Every supersession records the old claim, new claim, reason, evidence, and authorizing decision. The new claim must be distinct, accepted or accepted by the same resolution, and traceable to evidence that establishes the stated superiority. Supersession preserves the old claim permanently and transitions it to `Superseded`.
