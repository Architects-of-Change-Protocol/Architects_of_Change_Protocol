## Declaration

This document records the formal ratification of the AOC Constitutional System as v1.0, the first complete sovereign constitutional baseline of the AOC Protocol.

AOC Constitution v1.0 is the authoritative foundation for all AOC Protocol governance, execution, and evolution. All constitutional artifacts declare v1.0. All future constitutional changes require governed amendment.

## Ratification Doctrine

- Complete ≠ Ratified: A complete Constitution is not automatically authoritative.
- Audited ≠ Ratified: An audited Constitution must still be formally adopted.
- Ratified ≠ Immutable: A ratified Constitution may evolve through governed amendment.
- v1.0 ≠ Final: v1.0 is the first sovereign baseline, not the end of constitutional evolution.

## Versioning Declaration

Development versions v2.0–v18.0 were construction-era constitutional development versions. AOC Constitution v1.0 is the first ratified sovereign baseline. Future versions after ratification follow: v1.1, v1.2, v1.3 for minor amendments; v2.0 for major constitutional amendments.
