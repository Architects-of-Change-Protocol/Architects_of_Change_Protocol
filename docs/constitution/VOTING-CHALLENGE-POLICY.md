# Voting Challenge Policy

**Constitution Version:** v15.0

## Valid challenge grounds

A voting challenge may be raised on the following grounds: Invalid Eligibility, Invalid Weight, Invalid Delegation, Improper Motion, Evidence Failure, Constitutional Conflict, Governance Conflict.
