# Standing Eligibility Policy

**Constitution Version:** v8.0

## Constitutional rule

No standing may become active without successful eligibility validation. Validation is deny-by-default: missing, expired, contradictory, or integrity-invalid evidence leaves standing in `Pending Validation` or causes suspension or revocation.

## Validation semantics

Validation must identify the participant, applicable standing, validator, evidence, result, time, and amendment authority. Renewal does not silently extend expired evidence. This policy defines constitutional requirements and does not implement identity, authentication, authorization, claims, or trust runtimes.
