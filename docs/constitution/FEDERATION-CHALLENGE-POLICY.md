# Federation Challenge Policy

**Constitution Version:** v18.0

## Purpose

Challenge policy governs the grounds on which a federation relationship or federation act may be challenged. Challenges must be grounded in a cataloged violation category.

## Valid challenge grounds

| Grounds | Description |
|---|---|
| Invalid Recognition | The recognition record is invalid, fraudulent, or lacks required evidence |
| Invalid Trust | The trust level assignment violates the trust policy |
| Invalid Delegation | The delegation record violates the delegation policy |
| Invalid Capability Sharing | The capability sharing record violates the capability policy |
| Governance Conflict | The federated governance act conflicts with local governance authority |
| Constitutional Conflict | The federation relationship conflicts with constitutional requirements |
| Sovereignty Violation | The federation act infringes upon the sovereignty of a participating system |
| Evidence Failure | Required evidence is missing, insufficient, or fraudulent |
