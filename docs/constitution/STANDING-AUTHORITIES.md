# Standing Authorities

**Constitution Version:** v12.0

## Constitutional rule

Only standing authorities recorded here may confer a constitutional right to participate. Catalog presence does not activate standing: the corresponding eligibility policy must be validated and the lifecycle ledger must reach `Active`.

## Catalog integrity

Standing IDs and eligibility-policy IDs are permanent and unique. Retirement preserves historical records. A canonical entry may change only through a ratified Type B or Type C amendment; creation, retirement, class changes, or expansion of delegation or representation require Type C.
